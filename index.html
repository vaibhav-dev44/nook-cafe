About The Nook Cafe

Your Cozy Escape in Pune

At The Nook Cafe, we celebrate the art of handcrafted food made with fresh, local ingredients. Pair it with our signature 100% Arabica coffee, sourced from the finest estates and roasted to perfection.

Whether you're grabbing a quick espresso or settling in for a leisurely brunch, our warm ambiance and attentive service make every visit special.
